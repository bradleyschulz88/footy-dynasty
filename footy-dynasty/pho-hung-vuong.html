Our Story

Cooking Saigon's
Finest Phở Since
The 90s

Born in the heart of Footscray, Phở Hùng Vương Saigon has been the quiet legend of Hopkins Street for over three decades. No gimmicks. No pretense. Just honest, soul-warming broth simmered for hours, served fast and served right.
